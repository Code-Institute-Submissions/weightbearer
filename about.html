Meet Weight Bearer

Aden, Vocals

A member of The Coriolis Effect from a few years back. If your in a band or promoter wanting to get us on a show Aden is your contact for bookings and promo work!

Ben G, Guitar

He was the lead guitarist in The Coriolis Effect a few years back and has written lyrics for a number of tracks from our upcoming release 'Watch the Collapse'

Ben P, Drums

You've seen the clips, this dude can hit dem tubs and he use to play in a little band called Azriel. Needless to say we are stoked to have him behind the kit!

Chris, Guitar

Everyone meet the creative powerhouse of Weight Bearer, our guitarist Chris. Riffs, riffs and more riffs, if you like em, he wrote em! Our forthcoming album 'Watch the Collapse' has been recorded, produced, mixed & mastered by him.

James, Bass

He was the bassist for The Coriolis Effect a few years back and is a big Preston North End Football Club fan! He is also responsible for some of our visual teasers.

Lek, Vocals

He use to play drums in the band DEATHTRIP and transitioned to vocals for the band Times Of Collapse . Bascially he likes to get angry and either hit things or shout at people (mic optional). He has also written alot of the lyrical concepts for 'Watch the Collapse'.
