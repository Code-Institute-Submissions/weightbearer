Aden - Vocals

Formerly a member of the Coriolis Effect, Aden brings his aggressive vocals into Weight Bearer, as well as promotional skills.

Ben G - Guitar

Formerly lead guitarist in the Coriolis Effect, Ben also contributes lyrics for a number of songs on latest record Watch the Collapse. Ben plays Ernie Ball strings on PRS, through a Marshall stack and Orange Terror head.

Ben P - Drums

Behind the drum kit is Ben, who previously handled percussion in the legendary Azrael. Ben plays Paiste drums and cymbals

Chris - Guitar

After dissolving Deathtrip and Times of Collapse, Chris started writing the majority of material for Weight Bearer. Chris produced, mixed and mastered Watch the Collapse, and plays Ibanez guitars through an Orange rig.

James - Bass

The third member of the Coriolis Effect in the Weight Bearer ranks, James handles bass, as well as producing visuals for the band. Ben plays Ibanez basses through a Sunn stack and an Orange Terror head.

Lek - Vocals

Previously the drummer in Deathtrip, Lek moved up front to take care of vocals in Times of Collapse and now contributes most of the lyrics for Weight Bearer.
